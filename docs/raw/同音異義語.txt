<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%90%8C%E9%9F%B3%E7%95%B0%E7%BE%A9%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>同音異義語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동음이의어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>란 <a href="/index.php?title=%E7%99%BC%E9%9F%B3&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="發音 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade62 unknown"><rb>發音</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>발음</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>은 같으나 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%84%8F%E5%91%B3&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="意味 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>意味</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의미</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>가 다른 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%96%AE%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="單語 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>單語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>단어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>를 말한다. <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>普通</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>보통</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 한 <a href="/wiki/%E8%A8%80%E8%AA%9E" title="言語"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>言語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>언어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>를 배울 때에는 이 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>同音異義語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동음이의어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>들의 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>各各</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>각각</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 다른 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>意味</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의미</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>集中的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>집중적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%B7%A5%E5%A4%AB&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="工夫 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade70 unknown"><rb>工夫</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>하게 된다.
</p>
