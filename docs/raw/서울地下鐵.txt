<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%84%9C%EC%9A%B8%E5%9C%B0%E4%B8%8B%E9%90%B5&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>서울<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>地下鐵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지하철</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <a href="/wiki/%EC%84%9C%EC%9A%B8%E7%89%B9%E5%88%A5%E5%B8%82" title="서울特別市">서울<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>特別市</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특별시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>와 그 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>近郊地域</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>근교지역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <a href="/wiki/%E5%9C%B0%E4%B8%8B%E9%90%B5" title="地下鐵"><ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>地下鐵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지하철</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>路線</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>노선</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>들을 말한다. 좁은 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>意味</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의미</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로는 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%84%9C%EC%9A%B8%E4%BA%A4%E9%80%9A%E5%85%AC%E7%A4%BE&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="서울交通公社 (없는 문서)">서울<ruby class="hanja grade60 unknown"><rb>交通公社</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>교통공사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>가 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>運營</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>운영</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>路線</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>노선</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>들만을 가리킨다.
</p>
<h2><span id="特徵(특징)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E7.89.B9.E5.BE.B5.28.ED.8A.B9.EC.A7.95.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特徵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%84%9C%EC%9A%B8%E5%9C%B0%E4%B8%8B%E9%90%B5&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 特徵(특징)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="計劃(계획)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E8.A8.88.E5.8A.83.28.EA.B3.84.ED.9A.8D.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>計劃</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>계획</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%84%9C%EC%9A%B8%E5%9C%B0%E4%B8%8B%E9%90%B5&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 計劃(계획)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>總</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>총</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 3<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>期</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 걸쳐 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>計劃</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>계획</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>·<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>建設</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>건설</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되었다. 1<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>期</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>地下鐵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지하철</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>計劃</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>계획</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 1~4<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>號線</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>호선</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, 2기가 5~8<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>號線</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>호선</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, 3기가 9~12<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>號線</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>호선</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>該當</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하며, 10~12<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>號線</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>호선</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>實際</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>실제</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>建設</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>건설</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되지 못했다.
</p>
