<h3><span id="右縱書(우종서)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.8F.B3.E7.B8.B1.E6.9B.B8.28.EC.9A.B0.EC.A2.85.EC.84.9C.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>右縱書</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>우종서</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E7%B8%B1%E6%9B%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 右縱書(우종서)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>文章</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>들이 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>右</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>左</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>좌</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>順序</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>순서</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 흐르는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>方向</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방향</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다. <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>傳統的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전통적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <a href="/wiki/%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96%E5%9C%88" title="漢字文化圈"><ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>漢字文化圈</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자문화권</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>文書</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문서</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되었으며, <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>現在</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>현재</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <a href="/wiki/%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC" title="日本"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>出版物</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>출판물</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된다.
</p>
<h3><span id="左縱書(좌종서)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.B7.A6.E7.B8.B1.E6.9B.B8.28.EC.A2.8C.EC.A2.85.EC.84.9C.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>左縱書</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>좌종서</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E7%B8%B1%E6%9B%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="부분 편집: 左縱書(좌종서)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
