<p><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>海雲臺區</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해운대구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>釜山廣域市</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>부산광역시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>區</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="名稱(명칭)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.90.8D.E7.A8.B1.28.EB.AA.85.EC.B9.AD.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>名稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%B5%B7%E9%9B%B2%E8%87%BA%E5%8D%80&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 名稱(명칭)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%B4%94%E8%87%B4%E9%81%A0&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="崔致遠 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade12 unknown"><rb>崔致遠</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>최치원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%86%AC%E6%9F%8F%EC%84%AC&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="冬柏섬 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade20 unknown"><rb>冬柏</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동백</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>섬</a> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>一帶</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>景致</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 보고 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>自身</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자신</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <a href="/index.php?title=%E8%99%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="號 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade60 unknown"><rb>號</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>호</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>인 ‘<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>海雲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해운</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>’을 따서 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%B5%B7%E9%9B%B2%E8%87%BA&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="海雲臺 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>海雲臺</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해운대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>라는 이름을 붙였다는 것에서 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>由來</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유래</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>했다. <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>實際</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>실제</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>冬柏</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동백</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>섬에는 ‘<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>海雲臺</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해운대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>’라 새겨진 바위가 있는데, 이게 <ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>崔致遠</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>최치원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 쓴 것이라 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>傳</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해진다.
</p>
