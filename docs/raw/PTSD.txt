<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=PTSD&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>PTSD란 <b><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>外傷後</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>외상후</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 스트레스 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>障礙</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>장애</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>이며 어떤 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>契機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>계기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 인하여 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>事故</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사고</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>나 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>事件</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사건</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 당할때 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>持續的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지속적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>精神的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정신적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>後遺症</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>후유증</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>意味</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의미</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.
약칭은 Post-traumatic stress disorder 의 약칭인 PTSD라고 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.
</p>
