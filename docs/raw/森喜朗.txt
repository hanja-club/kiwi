<p><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>森喜朗</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>삼희랑</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은, 2000<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>年腦硬塞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년뇌경색</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>急死</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>급사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한<ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>小渕惠三</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>소연혜삼</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의뒤를이어<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>內閣總理大臣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>내각총리대신</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>座</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>좌</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>就任</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>취임</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하였다. <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>就任</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>취임</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>經緯</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경위</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는, <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>森喜朗本人</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>삼희랑본인</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>包含</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>포함</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>自民黨內</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자민당내</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>有力議員</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유력의원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>5<ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>名</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>靑木幹雄</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>청목간웅</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>村上正邦</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>촌상정방</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>野中廣務</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>야중광무</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>龜井靜香</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구정정향</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의"<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>密室談合</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>밀실담합</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>"인데, 이로<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>因</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>當時相當</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>당시상당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>批判</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>비판</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을받았다.

<p><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>森當時總理</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>삼당시총리</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는, <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>神道政治聯盟</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신도정치연맹</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>懇談會</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>간담회</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>出席</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>출석</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여, 2005<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>5<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>月</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>월</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>15<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>日以下</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일이하</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>와같은<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>發言</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>발언</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을하였다.

grade40"><rb>考慮</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>고려</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하였을때큰<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>問題</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문제</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는없다, <ruby class="hanja
