<h2><span id="槪要"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81"><a href="/wiki/%E6%A7%AA%E8%A6%81" title="槪要"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E8%A4%87%E5%90%88%E9%83%A8%E4%BD%8D%E7%97%9B%E7%97%87%E7%97%87%E5%80%99%E7%BE%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>複合部位痛症症候群</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>복합부위통증증후군</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E8%BA%AB%E9%AB%94&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="身體 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade62 unknown"><rb>身體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <a href="/index.php?title=%E4%B8%80%E9%83%A8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="一部 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade62 unknown"><rb>一部</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%BF%80%E7%97%9B&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="激痛 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>激痛</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>격통</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이 <a href="/index.php?title=%E7%99%BC%E7%94%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="發生 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade62 unknown"><rb>發生</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>발생</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>하는<a href="/index.php?title=%E4%B8%8D%E6%B2%BB%E7%97%85&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="不治病 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>不治病</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>불치병</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>을<a href="/index.php?title=%E6%84%8F%E5%91%B3&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="意味 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>意味</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의미</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>한다. <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>英略稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영략칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 CRPS 이다.
</p>
