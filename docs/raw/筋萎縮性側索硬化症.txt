<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E7%AD%8B%E8%90%8E%E7%B8%AE%E6%80%A7%E5%81%B4%E7%B4%A2%E7%A1%AC%E5%8C%96%E7%97%87&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/index.php?title=%E9%9A%A8%E6%84%8F%E7%AD%8B&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="隨意筋 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>隨意筋</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수의근</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>을 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%88%B6%E5%BE%A1&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="制御 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>制御</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>하는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E7%A5%9E%E7%B6%93%E7%B4%B0%E8%83%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="神經細胞 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>神經細胞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신경세포</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>가 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%B6%88%E6%BB%85&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="消滅 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>消滅</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>소멸</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>되는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E4%B8%8D%E6%B2%BB%E7%97%85&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="不治病 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>不治病</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>불치병</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> 이며 나중에는 <a href="/wiki/%E6%AD%BB%E4%BA%A1" title="死亡"><ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>死亡</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사망</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>으로 이어진다. <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%A3%A8%EA%B2%8C%EB%A6%AD%E7%97%85&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="루게릭病 (없는 문서)">루게릭<ruby class="hanja grade60 unknown"><rb>病</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>병</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이라고도 부른다.
<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>略稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>약칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 ALS이다. 
</p>
