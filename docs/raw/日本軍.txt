<h2><span id="槪要"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81"><a href="/wiki/%E6%A7%AA%E8%A6%81" title="槪要"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC%E8%BB%8D&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本軍</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본군</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은、<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>廣義</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>광의</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로는<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>軍隊</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>군대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>라는<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>意味</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의미</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이나、<ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>狹義及一般的定義</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>협의급일반적정의</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로는<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>第二次世界大戰時日本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제이차세계대전시일본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>保持</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>보지</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>했던<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>軍隊</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>군대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하며、<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>本項</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>본항</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서는이<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本軍</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본군</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>對</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>詳述</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>상술</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다。<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>別稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>별칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로는<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>皇軍</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>황군</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>、<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>官軍等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>관군등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이있다。
