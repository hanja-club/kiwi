<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%A4%A7%E9%98%AA%E5%B8%82&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><b><ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>大阪市</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대판시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>는 <a href="/wiki/%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC" title="日本"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%A4%A7%E9%98%AA%E5%BA%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="大阪府 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade12 unknown"><rb>大阪府</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대판부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에 있는 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>都市</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>도시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>府廳</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>부청</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>所在地</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>소재지</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p><p><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>表記</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>當初</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>당초</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> “<ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>大坂</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대판</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>”이 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>一般的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일반적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이었지만, “<ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>坂</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>판</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>”의 글<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>分解</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>분해</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하면 “땅(<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>土</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>토</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>)에 휜다(<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>反</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>)”라고 읽혀 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>財數</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>재수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 없다고 하여, <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>江戶時代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>강호시대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 무렵부터 “<ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>大阪</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대판</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>”라고도 쓰게 되어, <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>明治時代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명치시대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에는 <ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>大阪</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대판</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 글<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>定着</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정착</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다. <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>一說</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일설</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 “<ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>坂</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>판</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>”로부터 “<ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>阪</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>판</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>”에의 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>變更</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>변경</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>明治新政府</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명치신정부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 “<ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>坂</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>판</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>”이 “<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>士</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>反</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다”, <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>卽</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>즉</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> “<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>武士</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>무사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>叛亂</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>반란</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하다”라고 읽을 수 있기 때문에 “<ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>坂</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>판</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>”의 글<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 싫어했다.
</p>
