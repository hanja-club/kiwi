<p><a href="/wiki/%E6%89%93%E5%AD%97%E6%A9%9F" title="打字機"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>打字機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>中</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 <a href="/wiki/%ED%95%9C%EA%B8%80" title="한글">한글</a>을 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>打字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 수 있는 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>種類</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>종류</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>對</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>說明</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>설명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.
</p>
<h2><span id="歷史(역사)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.AD.B7.E5.8F.B2.28.EC.97.AD.EC.82.AC.29"><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>歷史</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%ED%95%9C%EA%B8%80%E6%89%93%E5%AD%97%E6%A9%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 歷史(역사)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/index.php?title=1914%E5%B9%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="1914年 (없는 문서)">1914<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> 이원익이 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>英文</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>打字機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <a href="/wiki/%ED%95%9C%EA%B8%80" title="한글">한글</a><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>活字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 붙여 만든 네벌<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>打字機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%AA%A8%EC%95%84%EC%93%B0%EA%B8%B0&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="모아쓰기 (없는 문서)">모아쓰기</a> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>打字機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>始初</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시초</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>라고 여겨진다.
</p>
