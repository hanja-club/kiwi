<html><head></head><body><div class="mw-parser-output"><h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%96%80%EB%8D%B0%EB%A0%88&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>얀데레(ヤンデレ)란 <a href="/wiki/%E6%97%A5%E8%AA%9E" class="mw-redirect" title="日語"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>日語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> “<span class="noruby"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>病</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>병</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>み</span>”(야미)와“デレ”(데레)의 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%90%88%E6%88%90%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="合成語 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade60 unknown"><rb>合成語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>합성어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이다. 이 デレ(데레)는 “でれでれ”(데레데레)라하는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E7%95%B0%E5%A7%93&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="異姓 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>異姓</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에게 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>熱中</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>열중</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어있는 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>狀態</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>상태</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>形容</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>형용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>日語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<a href="/index.php?title=%E5%89%AF%E8%A9%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="副詞 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>副詞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>부사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>由來</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유래</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.
</p><p>이의<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特徵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>簡潔</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>간결</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>히<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>說明</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>설명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하자면, “<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>特定相對</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특정상대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>對</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>好意</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>호의</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>過</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>과</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>함에따른 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>病的精神狀態</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>병적정신상태</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>”이다. <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>主</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>주</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%89%B5%E4%BD%9C%E7%89%A9&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="創作物 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>創作物</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>창작물</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>架空人物</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가공인물</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>發見</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>발견</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되나, <a href="/index.php?title=%E7%8F%BE%E5%AF%A6&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="現實 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade52 unknown"><rb>現實</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>현실</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에서도 “얀데레”를 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>自處</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자처</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 사람이 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>間或</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>간혹</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 있으며, 이<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>文書</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문서</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 이에<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>關</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>관</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해서도 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>後述</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>후술</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.
</p>
<h2><span id="定義(정의)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.AE.9A.E7.BE.A9.28.EC.A0.95.EC.9D.98.29"><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>定義</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정의</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%96%80%EB%8D%B0%EB%A0%88&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 定義(정의)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>얀데레에 하나의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>正確</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정확</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>定義</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정의</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 붙이는것은 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>甚</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>심</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>히어려우나, <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>代表的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대표적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特徵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로는 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>以下</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이하</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>와 같은것이 있다.
</p>
<ul><li><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>本來</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>본래</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>性格</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>성격</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>穩和</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>온화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하며, <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>內向的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>내향적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>境遇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 많다.</li>
<li><ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>上記</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>상기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>性格</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>성격</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>因</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%88%80%E5%BF%83&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="戀心 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>戀心</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>연심</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>對象</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에게 쉽게 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>接近</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>접근</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하지 못하며, <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>戀愛關係</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>연애관계</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>膠着狀態</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>교착상태</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>.</li>
<li><a href="/index.php?title=%E8%85%A6&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="腦 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>腦</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>뇌</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>內</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>내</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<a href="/index.php?title=%E5%A6%84%E6%83%B3&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="妄想 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>妄想</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>망상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>激</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>격</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하며, 홀로 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>腦內</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>뇌내</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>假想戀愛</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가상련애</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>發展</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>발전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>시키는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>境遇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가많다.</li>
<li><ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>閾値</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>宏壯</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>굉장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>히낮으며, <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>戀心</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>연심</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>對象</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>日常的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일상적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이며 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>些少</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사소</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>行動</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>행동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에도 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>過敏</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>과민</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>히<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>反應</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>반응</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.</li>
<li><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>獨占慾</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>독점욕</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>强</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>강</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하며, <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>異姓</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>戀心</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>연심</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>對象</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에게 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>接近</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>접근</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>境遇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 반드시 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>排除</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>배제</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하고 싶어한다.</li>
<li>쉽게 <a href="/index.php?title=%E8%87%AA%E6%9A%B4%E8%87%AA%E6%A3%84&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="自暴自棄 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade30 unknown"><rb>自暴自棄</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자포자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>狀態</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>상태</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 빠지며, 이<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>境遇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>自他</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자타</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>모두에게 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>害</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>加</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하고만다.</li></ul>
<h2><span id="現實(현실)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E7.8F.BE.E5.AF.A6.28.ED.98.84.EC.8B.A4.29"><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>現實</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>현실</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%96%80%EB%8D%B0%EB%A0%88&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 現實(현실)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>現實</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>현실</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에있어서의 “얀데레”는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>深刻</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>심각</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <a href="/index.php?title=%E7%B2%BE%E7%A5%9E%E7%97%85&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="精神病 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>精神病</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정신병</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이다. <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>現實</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>현실</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 얀데레를 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>自處</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자처</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는, <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>或</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>혹</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 얀데레로 불리는 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>人物</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인물</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 흔히 <a href="/index.php?title=%E7%B2%BE%E7%A5%9E%E7%97%85%E8%B3%AA&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="精神病質 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>精神病質</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정신병질</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>을지니며, <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>其他</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기타</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <a href="/index.php?title=%E8%89%B2%E6%83%85%E5%A6%84%E6%83%B3&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="色情妄想 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>色情妄想</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>색정망상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>, <a href="/index.php?title=%E4%B8%8D%E8%B2%9E%E5%A6%84%E6%83%B3&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="不貞妄想 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>不貞妄想</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>부정망상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>精神病</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정신병</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을지닌다.
