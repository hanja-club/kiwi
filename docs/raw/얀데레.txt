<h2><span id="特徵(특징)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E7.89.B9.E5.BE.B5.28.ED.8A.B9.EC.A7.95.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特徵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%96%80%EB%8D%B0%EB%A0%88&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 特徵(특징)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>얀데레는 누군가를 극적으로 사랑해서 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%9F%B7%E7%9D%80&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="執着 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>執着</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>집착</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>,<a href="/index.php?title=%EC%8A%A4%ED%86%A0%ED%82%B9&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="스토킹 (없는 문서)">스토킹</a>,<a href="/index.php?title=%E9%87%8D%E7%8A%AF%E7%BD%AA&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="重犯罪 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>重犯罪</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중범죄</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 상대에게 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>精神的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정신적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>,<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>肉體的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>육체적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>苦痛</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>고통</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 주는 사람을 말한다.
</p>
