<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E4%B8%BB%E7%A5%88%E7%A6%B1%E6%96%87&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>主祈禱文</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>주기도문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>希</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>희</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>:Κυριακή προσευχή, <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>羅</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>나</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>:Oratio Dominica<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>或</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>혹</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은Paster Noster, <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>英</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>:Lord's Prayer)는, <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%9F%BA%E7%9D%A3%E6%95%8E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="基督敎 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>基督敎</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기독교</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의<ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>祈禱文</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기도문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%98%88%EC%88%98_%EA%B7%B8%EB%A6%AC%EC%8A%A4%EB%8F%84&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="예수 그리스도 (없는 문서)">예수 그리스도</a>(Jesus Christ)<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>自身</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자신</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이<ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>弟子</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>들에게 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>直接敎示</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>직접교시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한<ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>祈禱文</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기도문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로서 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%96%B0%E7%B4%84%E8%81%96%E7%B6%93&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="新約聖經 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>新約聖經</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신약성경</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>記錄</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기록</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되있으며, <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>基督敎敎派</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기독교교파</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>太半</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>태반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 외우는<ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>祈禱文</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기도문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다. 
</p>
<h2><span id="原文(원문)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.8E.9F.E6.96.87.28.EC.9B.90.EB.AC.B8.29"><ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>原文</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E4%B8%BB%E7%A5%88%E7%A6%B1%E6%96%87&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 原文(원문)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>主祈禱文</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>주기도문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>原文</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 그리스<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로되어있다. <br>
πάτερ ἡμῶν ὁ ἐν τοῖς οὐρανοῖς<br>
ἁγιασθήτω τὸ ὄνομά σου<br>
ἐλθέτω ἡ βασιλεία σου<br>
γενηθήτω τὸ θέλημά σου ὡς ἐν οὐρανῷ καὶ ἐπὶ γῆς<br>
τὸν ἄρτον ἡμῶν τὸν ἐπιούσιον δὸς ἡμῖν σήμερον<br>
καὶ ἄφες ἡμῖν τὰ ὀφειλήματα ἡμῶν ὡς καὶ ἡμεῖς ἀφήκαμεν τοῖς ὀφειλέταις ἡμῶν<br>
καὶ μὴ εἰσενέγκῃς ἡμᾶς εἰς πειρασμόν ἀλλὰ ῥῦσαι ἡμᾶς ἀπὸ τοῦ πονηροῦ<br>
</p>
<h2><span id="譯文(역문)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E8.AD.AF.E6.96.87.28.EC.97.AD.EB.AC.B8.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>譯文</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E4%B8%BB%E7%A5%88%E7%A6%B1%E6%96%87&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 譯文(역문)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="라틴語(어)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".EB.9D.BC.ED.8B.B4.E8.AA.9E.28.EC.96.B4.29">라틴<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E4%B8%BB%E7%A5%88%E7%A6%B1%E6%96%87&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="부분 편집: 라틴語(어)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>1592<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%95%8E%E7%9A%87&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="敎皇 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>敎皇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>교황</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>클레먼트8<ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>世</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>세</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>依</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>公布</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공포</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된, Vulgata Clementina에<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>依</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>譯文</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>以下</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이하</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>와같다. <br>
pater noster qui es in cælis<br>
sanctificetur nomen tuum<br>
adveniat regnum tuum<br>
fiat voluntas tua sicut in cælo et in terra<br>
panem nostrum supersubstantialem da nobis hodie<br>
et dimitte nobis debita nostra sicut et nos dimittimus debitoribus nostris<br>
et ne nos inducas in tentationem sed libera nos a malo<br>
1960<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>年代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>연대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>까지, <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%A4%A9%E4%B8%BB%E6%95%8E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="天主敎 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade70 unknown"><rb>天主敎</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>천주교</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에서<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>通用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>통용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되었던 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>譯文</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>以下</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이하</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>와같다. <br>
Pater noster qui es in caelis<br>
sanctificetur nomen tuum<br>
adveniat regnum tuum<br>
fiat voluntas tua sicut in caelo et in terra<br>
Panem nostrum cotidianum da nobis hodie<br>
et dimitte nobis debita nostra<br>
sicut et nos dimittimus debitoribus nostris<br>
et ne nos inducas in tentationem<br>
sed libera nos a malo
</p>
<h3><span id="韓國語(한국어)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E9.9F.93.E5.9C.8B.E8.AA.9E.28.ED.95.9C.EA.B5.AD.EC.96.B4.29"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>韓國語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한국어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E4%B8%BB%E7%A5%88%E7%A6%B1%E6%96%87&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="부분 편집: 韓國語(한국어)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>하늘에 계신 우리 아버지여<br>
이름을 거룩히 여김을 받으시오시며<br>
나라이 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>臨</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>임</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하시오며<br>
뜻이 하늘에서 이루어진 것같이 땅에서도 이루어지이다<br>
오늘 우리에게 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>日用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>糧食</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>양식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 주시옵고<br>
우리가 우리에게 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>罪</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>죄</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 지은 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>者</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>赦</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 준 것같이 우리 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>罪</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>죄</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>赦</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 주시옵고<br>
우리를 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>試驗</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시험</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 들게 하지 마시옵고 다만 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>惡</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>악</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>救</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하시옵소서<br>
나라와 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>權勢</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>권세</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>와 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>榮光</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영광</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 아버지께 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>永遠</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>히 있사옵나이다. 아멘. <br>
</p>
<h3><span id="英語(영어)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E8.8B.B1.E8.AA.9E.28.EC.98.81.EC.96.B4.29"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>英語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E4%B8%BB%E7%A5%88%E7%A6%B1%E6%96%87&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="부분 편집: 英語(영어)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Our Father who art in heaven<br>
Hallowed be thy name<br>
Thy kingdom come<br>
Thy will be done on earth as it is in heaven<br>
Give us this day our daily bread<br>
And forgive us our debts <br>
As we forgive our debtors<br>
And lead us not into temptation<br>
But deliver us from evil<br>
For thine is the kingdom, and the power, and the glory, for ever and ever. Amen. <br>
</p>
<h3><span id="日本語(일본어)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.97.A5.E6.9C.AC.E8.AA.9E.28.EC.9D.BC.EB.B3.B8.EC.96.B4.29"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E4%B8%BB%E7%A5%88%E7%A6%B1%E6%96%87&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="부분 편집: 日本語(일본어)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>天</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>천</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>にまします<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>我</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>아</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>らの<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>父</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>よ<br>

<ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>御国</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>어국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>を<ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>来</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>내</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>たらせたまえ<br>

<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>我</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>아</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>らの<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>日用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>の<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>糧</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>양</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>を、<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>今日</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>금일</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>も<ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>与</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>여</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>えたまえ<br>
<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>我</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>아</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>らに<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>罪</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>죄</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>を<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>犯</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>범</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>す<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>者</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>を、<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>我</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>아</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>らが<ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>赦</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>す<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>如</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>여</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>く<br>
<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>我</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>아</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>らの<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>罪</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>죄</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>も<ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>赦</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>したまえ<br>
<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>我</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>아</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>らを<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>試</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>みに<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>合</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>합</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>わせず、<ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>悪</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>악</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>より<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>救</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>い<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>出</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>출</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>したまえ<br>
